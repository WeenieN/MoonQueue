Why did you pick BSC for the launch?

Our token model encourages DEX trading. 

While we consider Ethereum to be far more popular, we think that the fees on the ETH chain are unfair for the small guy.  

We will create a wrapped version of Weenie Inu on ETH, which will be pegged to the price of BSC Weenie Inu, effectively expanding to both chains, however, we hold that back for the time being hoping for ETH to address the scalability issues it faces.
